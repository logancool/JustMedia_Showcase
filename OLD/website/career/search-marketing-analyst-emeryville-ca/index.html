[ Search Marketing Analyst – Emeryville, CA ]

The role of an SEM Analyst is to prepare, analyze, report on, and optimize clients’ search engine marketing campaigns.

Just Media’s Emeryville, CA office is hiring whip-smart, self-motivated Search Engine Marketing Analyst who enjoys multi-tasking, and has the ability to think laterally, quickly, and independently.

Essential Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
-Monitor campaign performance
-Troubleshoot issues/concerns and work with online partners to resolve as needed.
-Assist in managing relationships with search engines and third-party partners
-Assist in client call preparation.  Attend client calls and meetings as required
-Develop keyword lists, write ad creatives, test landing page messaging, landing page layout, audience segmentation, placements, networks, and 3rd-party tools.
-Produce and analyze weekly, monthly, and quarterly campaign reports measuring performance and effectiveness of search campaigns. 

Need to Haves:
-BS/BA degree
-Experience working in Google AdWords and Bing Ads
-Excellent analytical skills, including the ability to analyze data, draw conclusions and develop recommendations
-Excellent verbal and written communication skills
-Ability to thrive in a flexible, team-based environment and ability to learn/adapt quickly
-Self-motivation and integrity
-Exceptional attention to detail with the ability to meet aggressive deadlines
-Working proficiency in MS Excel (pivot tables and vlookups) & MS Powerpoint (make cohesive presentations showcasing client recommendations)

Nice to Haves:
– Agency Experience
– Google AdWords certification
– B2B Industry Experience
– International SEM Experience

Just Media, Inc. an equal opportunity employer, offers an exciting opportunity for qualified career-motivated professionals. We provide competitive compensation and the chance to work with talented people in a winning, successful, and pleasant environment. We offer many premium benefits which currently include, but are not limited to, 401(k), medical and dental.

Interested candidates who fit the description are encouraged to respond to hr@justmedia.com.
Applicants MUST respond with the following information to be considered:
- Resume with cover letter explaining your qualifications for this position
- Office location you are applying for
- Salary Range Requirements
